# Change project pod limit

[Suggest Edits](/edit/change-project-pod-limit)## Overview


If you are a [project owner](projects#project-roles), follow these steps to change the maximum total number of [pods](indexes#pods-pod-types-and-pod-sizes) in your project.


## Change project pod limit in console


1. Access the [Pinecone Console](https://app.pinecone.io).
2. Click **Settings** in the left menu.
3. In the **Settings** view, click the **PROJECTS** tab.
4. Next to the project you want to update, click ![pencil icon](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/pinecone-io/img/main/edit-icon.png).
5. Under **Pod Limit**, enter the new number of pods.
6. Click **SAVE CHANGES**.
