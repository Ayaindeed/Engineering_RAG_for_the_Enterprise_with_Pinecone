# Datadog

[Suggest Edits](/edit/datadog)## Overview


This topic describes how to use Datadog to monitor your Pinecone vector database.


## Set up your Pinecone integration on Datadog.


After you [install a Datadog agent](https://docs.datadoghq.com/agent/), follow these steps to set up your Pinecone integration:


1. Log in to [Datadoghq.com](https://datadoghq.com).
2. Go to [Datadoghq.com](https://datadoghq.com) > [Integrations](https://datadoghq.com/integrations).
3. In the search bar, enter 'Pinecone'.
4. Click **Pinecone Integration**.
5. Click **Install**.
6. Under **Pinecone Accounts**, click **Add new**.
7. Enter the [project ID](projects#project-id), [project environment](projects#project-environment), and [API key](projects#api-keys) for your project.
8. In the left navigation menu, click **Dashboards**.
9. Under **All Dashboards**, click **Pinecone Overview**. This dashboard shows basic monitoring data for your Pinecone vector database.


## Create Datadog monitors for your Pinecone vector database.


After you set up your Pinecone integration on Datadog, you can create monitors for your Pinecone vector database.


1. Log in to [Datadoghq.com](https://datadoghq.com).
2. Go to [Datadoghq.com](https://datadoghq.com) > [Integrations](https://datadoghq.com/integrations).
3. In the search bar, enter 'Pinecone'.
4. Click **Pinecone Integration**.
5. Click the **Monitors** tab.
6. [Configure your Datadog monitor](https://docs.datadoghq.com/monitors/configuration/?tab=thresholdalert) using [Pinecone's metrics](monitoring#available-metrics).
